• The Edinburgh Post-Natal Depression Scale (EPDS) is a 10 question self-rating scale used for postpartum depression screening.

  • 01. Able to laugh and see the funny side of things
  • 02. Looked forward with enjoyment to things
  • 03. Blamed myself unnecessarily when things went wrong
  • 04. Anxious or worried for no good reason
  • 05. Felt scared or panicky for no very good reason
  • 06. Things have been getting on top of me
  • 07. So unhappy that I have had difficulty sleeping
  • 08. Felt sad or miserable
  • 09. So unhappy that I have been crying
  • 10. Thought of harming myself has occurred to me
