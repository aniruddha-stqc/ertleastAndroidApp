• The Modified Alvarado Appendicitis Score is a numerical scoring system used to diagnose appendicitis in an adult population when the differential white blood cell count is not available.

  • Migratory RLQ abdominal pain
  • Loss of appetite
  • Nausea/vomiting
  • RLQ tenderness
  • RLQ rebound tenderness
  • Fever > 37.3°C (99.14°F)
  • Leukocytosis > 10k WBCs
  • Additional sign (e.g. cough test, Rovsing's sign, or rectal tenderness)
